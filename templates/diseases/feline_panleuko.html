Feline Panleukopenia (Cat Distemper) 🐱

Cause: A severe viral infection in cats that destroys white blood cells and weakens immunity.

⚠️ Symptoms:
  • High fever, vomiting, and diarrhea
  • Loss of appetite and dehydration
  • Sudden collapse in kittens
🩺 When to see vet:

Don’t wait — dehydration can kill fast. Needs IV fluids, antibiotics, and supportive care.

💡 Prevention:
  • Keep cat vaccinations up to date
  • Disinfect litter boxes regularly
