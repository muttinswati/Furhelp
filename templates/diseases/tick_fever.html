Tick Fever (Ehrlichiosis) 🕷️

Cause: Spread through tick bites carrying Ehrlichia bacteria.

⚠️ Symptoms:
  • High fever and loss of appetite
  • Pale gums and nose bleeding
  • Swollen lymph nodes
  • Lethargy or reluctance to walk
🩺 When to visit vet:

Visit a vet immediately if your pet has fever for more than 2 days or pale gums — untreated, it can become fatal in a week.

💡 Prevention:
  • Use tick prevention collars or sprays
  • Regularly check fur for ticks, especially behind ears and paws
