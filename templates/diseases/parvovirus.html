Canine Parvovirus 💩

Cause: A highly contagious viral disease that attacks a dog’s intestines and immune system.

⚠️ Symptoms:
  • Severe vomiting and bloody diarrhea
  • Rapid dehydration
  • Foul smell from stool
  • Extreme weakness
🚨 Warning:

Parvo kills puppies in 2–3 days if untreated. Immediate vet care and IV fluids are life-saving.

💡 Prevention:
  • Vaccinate your dog on time (core vaccine)
  • Keep unvaccinated pups away from stray dogs
