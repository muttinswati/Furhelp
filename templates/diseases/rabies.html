Rabies in Dogs and Cats 🐕🐈

Cause: A fatal viral infection affecting the brain and nervous system, transmitted through bites or saliva of infected animals.

⚠️ Symptoms:
  • Excessive drooling
  • Unusual aggression or fear
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Seizures or paralysis
💡 Important:

Rabies is 100% fatal once symptoms appear, but entirely preventable through timely **vaccination**. If bitten by any animal, **seek a vet immediately**.

🩺 What to do:
  • Do NOT try to treat at home.
  • Wash the bite area with soap and water immediately.
  • Visit a vet or doctor for post-exposure vaccination.
