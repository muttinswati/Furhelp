Leptospirosis 🧬

Cause: A bacterial infection spread through contaminated water, soil, or urine of infected animals.

⚠️ Symptoms:
  • Fever and muscle pain
  • Yellowing of eyes (jaundice)
  • Vomiting and loss of appetite
  • Increased thirst and urination
🩺 Important:

Leptospirosis can spread to humans. If suspected, visit a vet immediately and avoid contact with pet urine.

💡 Prevention:
  • Clean pet bowls daily
  • Keep pets away from stagnant water
  • Get annual vaccination
