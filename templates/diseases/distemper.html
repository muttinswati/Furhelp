Canine Distemper 🤧

Cause: A contagious viral disease affecting a dog’s respiratory, digestive, and nervous systems.

⚠️ Symptoms:
  • Coughing and nasal discharge
  • Loss of appetite and fever
  • Eye discharge and seizures
  • Paw pad thickening (hard pad disease)
🩺 Important:

No home treatment. Needs immediate vet attention — untreated, it causes neurological damage or death.

💡 Prevention:
  • Regular vaccination is key
  • Keep infected dogs isolated
