Ringworm (Fungal Infection) 🦠

Cause: A contagious fungal infection that affects the skin, hair, and claws of pets — not worms!

⚠️ Symptoms:
  • Round bald patches with dry, scaly skin
  • Itching and redness
  • Brittle or broken fur
🩺 Important:

Ringworm spreads to humans too. Always wear gloves while treating infected pets.

💡 Prevention:
  • Disinfect bedding and grooming tools
  • Keep infected pets isolated
